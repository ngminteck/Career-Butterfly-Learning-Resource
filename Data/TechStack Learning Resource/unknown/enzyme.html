In a nutshell, enzyme technology is a subfield of biotechnology that focuses on utilizing enzymes as biocatalysts to develop new processes for manufacturing both bulk and high-value products. These products span various domains, including food (e.g., bread, cheese, beer, vinegar), fine chemicals (such as amino acids and vitamins), and pharmaceuticals1.
