Power BI is a comprehensive suite of software services, apps, and connectors that collaboratively transform disparate data sources into cohesive, visually engaging, and interactive insights. Whether your data originates from an Excel spreadsheet or a combination of cloud-based and on-premises hybrid data warehouses, Power BI enables easy connectivity, visualization, discovery, and sharing of critical information with anyone you choose 1.
