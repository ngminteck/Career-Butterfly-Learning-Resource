The term ERNIE refers to two distinct frameworks, each with its own context:

  1. ERNIE (Enhanced Representation through kNowledge IntEgration):

    • ERNIE is a language representation model that enhances traditional language models by incorporating knowledge.
    • Inspired by the masking strategy of BERT, ERNIE is designed to learn language representations enhanced by knowledge masking strategies, including entity-level masking and phrase-level masking1.
    • It aims to improve language understanding and generation tasks by integrating external knowledge into the model.
